With nearly 40 years of industrial experience in colouring plastics, especially polyolefins, we have seen and solved most problems.

Our advice is impartial and unbiased because we are not affiliated with any colour or masterbatch suppliers.

If you are a masterbatch manufacturer, we can assist you with -

  • Choosing a pigment palette
  • Heavy Metal replacement
  • Compliance with food contact and toy standards
  • Cost reduction

If you are a manufacturer and use masterbatches and other plastics colourants, we can help you to -

  • Organise a successful tender
  • Improve the environmental status of your products
  • Rationalise inventory
  • Develop a standard range of colours

We are based in Melbourne, Australia but can visit you wherever you are. To find out more, email jonpaul@igreen.net
